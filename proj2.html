Mechatronic Hand

Class: ME 122 - Processing of Materials in Manufacturing

Role: Mechanical Engineer

Designed a mechanical prosthesis for patients with disabilities driven to accomplish performace intensive activites.

Did basic calculations (stress, force, impact), modeling, and FEA of components to ensure plausible material choice and drive design decisions.

This design makes use of different modular parts in order to address wear and repeated use applications of high performace activities. Since individuals are all different and each activity will result in different mecahnical interactions, this modularity provides a way to address this design space.

Manufacturing methods discussed for this part include metal injection molding of inner structural components and a plastic overmoulded shell to cover these parts.
