360o Action Camera

360o Action Camera

Design Challenge

I designed a 360o action camera for a design challenge interview. The design challenge was open-ended with the sole design goal of having a 360 degree horizontal field of view.

I approached this challenge by using optics and thermals as my guiding principles. I wanted to design an action camera that did not capture the user's helmet when in use and did research on how field of view effects camera sensor and lens choices. As for thermals, the action camera is used in both extreme temperatures (cold and hot). The camera also has to write tons of data when recroding 4k video, therefore the electronics produce a lot of heat and that heat has to be taken away for functional purposes.
